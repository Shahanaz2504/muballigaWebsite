Why This Course Matters

Seeking Knowledge Is a Path to Paradise

“Whoever follows a path in pursuit of knowledge, Allah will make easy for him a path to Paradise.”

Sahih Muslim, 2699

A muballigha is a woman who conveys — tabligh — authentic Islamic teaching to others. The tradition goes back to the earliest generation of Muslim women: Aisha (رضي الله عنها) narrated thousands of hadith and taught scholars of her time, and countless women since have carried that same responsibility of passing on sound knowledge. This course exists to train a new generation of women to do the same — learning directly from the Qur'an and Sunnah, in their own language, so they can teach with confidence.

Elevates Your Status

“Allah will raise those who have believed among you and those who were given knowledge, by degrees.” (Surah Al-Mujadila 58:11)

Brings You Closer to Allah

Understanding what you recite and why you worship deepens every act of ibadah and strengthens iman.

A Better, Righteous Life

Practical fiqh and akhlaq give you clarity for the everyday decisions of worship, family and daily living.

Guide Your Family & Society

“Convey from me, even if one verse.” (Sahih al-Bukhari) — a trained muballigha becomes a source of guidance at home and in her community.

Curriculum

Subjects Taught

A well-rounded foundation covering recitation, memorisation, hadith, jurisprudence and character.

Quran Recitation

Learn to recite the Qur'an correctly and beautifully, building a personal connection with the words of Allah.

Tajweed

Master the rules of correct Arabic pronunciation so every letter and word is recited as it was revealed.

Surah Memorisation

Memorise essential surahs for daily prayer and personal recitation, with a structured revision routine.

Hadees

Study authentic sayings and traditions of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ to understand the Sunnah in daily life.

Fiqh

Practical Islamic jurisprudence — purification, prayer, fasting and family matters — explained clearly.

Akhlaq (Islamic Manners)

Cultivate the character and everyday etiquette exemplified by the Prophet ﷺ.
